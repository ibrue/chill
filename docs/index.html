Chill

Smart fan control for Apple Silicon. Keep your Mac cooler and quieter under load.

Download for Apple Silicon View on GitHub
brew install --cask ibrue/tap/chill Signed with Developer ID and notarized by Apple.

What it does

Cool earlier

Shifts Apple's fan curve 4°C or 8°C earlier, or go full Performance, all from the menu bar.

Live monitoring

CPU and GPU temperatures, per-fan RPM, and power draw at a glance.

Per-app rules

Switch profiles automatically when demanding apps launch.

Auto updates

Quietly keeps itself up to date with signed, notarized releases.

Requirements

macOS 14 or later on an Apple Silicon Mac (M1 through M4). Chill installs a small privileged helper to control the fans, so you'll be asked for your admin password once during install.

Chill is open source. github.com/ibrue/chill
